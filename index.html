About Dissonance

Dissonance is a platform-puzzle game currently being developed by Zi Li as her Masters thesis. In Dissonance, the players plays as Lian who has been split into two 2D characters: Li and An. Players must navigate Li and An through various puzzles and recombine them into Lian.
